1. Cook and stir onions in dressing in large skillet on medium heat 5 min. or until crisp-tender.
  2. Add meat; cook on medium-high heat until evenly browned, stirring frequently.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and tomatoes. Bring to boil; simmer on medium-low heat 15 min., stirring frequently.
  4. Meanwhile cook spaghetti as directed on package, omitting salt
  5. Remove sauce from heat. Add cream cheese, stir until melted.
  6. Drain pasta; place on platter. Top with sauce and parmesan
