layout: recipe
servings: "12"
ingredients:
  - name: "(200 g) flour"
    quantity: "1.5"
    unit: "cups"
  - name: "(10 g) baking powder"
    quantity: "3"
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "(25 g) sugar"
    quantity: "2.5"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "(2g) salt"
    quantity: "1/2"
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "(400 g) milk"
    quantity: "1¾"
    unit: "cups"
  - name: ""
    quantity: "2"
    unit: "eggs"
  - name: "vanilla extract"
    quantity: "½"
    unit: "tsp"

---
Sift together the flour & baking powder and stir in the salt and sugar.

Make a well and slowly add the eggs and milk and vanilla extract.

Cook in a well greased pan over medium low, adding 4 tbsp batter per pancake and regreasing after each.
