Add lemon zest, cream, and sugar to a pan. Bring to a boil.

When exactly 2 cups remain (8-12 minutes), turn off the heat, add the lemon juice and let cool for 20 minutes.

Strain to remove skin and zest and portion into 6 servings.

Chill for ~3 hours.
