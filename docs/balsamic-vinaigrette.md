---
title: "Balsamic vinaigrette"
layout: recipe
servings: "2"
ingredients:
  - name: "clove garlic, minced"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "large"
  - name: "honey"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "dijon mustard"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "salt"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "black pepper"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "balsamic vinegar"
    quantity: "1/3"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "extra virgin olive oil"
    quantity: "1/2"
    unit: "cup"

---
In a jar, combine honey, mustard, salt, pepper, and garlic with balsamic vinegar. Stir to combine.

Add about half the olive oil and shake vigorously until emulsified. Repeat with the second half. Store in fridge.

If not using a jar, slowly stream in oil while whisking vigorously.
