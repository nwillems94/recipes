Combine 1 cup flour, 1 cup bread flour, yeast, & salt and stir to combine

Heat milk to lukewarm. Add and whisk egg

Add milk egg mixture and combine into a shaggy dough. Let hydrate for 20 minutes

Add raisins. Knead dough, adding bread flour gradually until the dough only slightly sticky. Let rise for 1 hour

Grease bread pan. Flatten dough and fold into thirds (dry side in). Stretch dough over itself and place into bread pan. Avoid raisins on the outer surface of the bread. Let rise 1 hour or overnight in the fridge.

Bake at 450F for 40 minutes
