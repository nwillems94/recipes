---
title: "Buns"
layout: recipe
servings: "8"
ingredients:
  - name: "(200 g) whole wheat flour"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "(350 g) bread flour"
    quantity: "2"
    unit: "cups"
  - name: "cup sugar"
    quantity: "1/8"
    unit: "(20 g)"
  - name: "yeast"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "salt"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "butter"
    quantity: "5"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "(150g) milk"
    quantity: "0.5"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "(250g) water"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: ""
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "egg"

---
Combine flour, sugar, yeast, & salt and stir to combine.

Add warm water and milk and combine until no wet spots remain.

Knead dough, slowly adding small pieces of butter. Continue kneading for 5 minutes after all the butter has been added. Let rise for 1 hour.

Form into 8 equally sized buns and place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Let rise for 1 hour.

Brush with beaten egg yolk and sesame seeds. Bake at 350F for 30 minutes, rotating half way through.
