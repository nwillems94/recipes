Preheat oven to 350°F.

In a large bowl, combine wet ingredients. 

In a separate large bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients (except chocolate chips).

Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and stir until just combined, then fold in chocolate chips, if using. 

In a well-greased muffin tin, divide batter into ~75 g portions. 

Bake for 18–22 minutes until a toothpick comes out mostly clean.
