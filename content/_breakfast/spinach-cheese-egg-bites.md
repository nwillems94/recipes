Pre-heat oven to 300°F.

Blend eggs, cottage cheese, cornstarch, salt, and pepper until completely smooth.

Stir cheese and finely chopped spinach into egg mixture.

Portion into a greased muffin tin about ¾ full. 
Place muffin tin in a larger pan, add hot water halfway up the sides. 
Bake 30–35 minutes, until just set.
