---
title: "Wilted romaine"
layout: recipe
ingredients:
  - name: "Romaine"
  - name: "Light soy sauce or shoyu"
  - name: "Oyster sauce"
  - name: "Garlic"

---
Cool down minced garlic until it begins to brown.

Add oyster sauce, soy sauce, and a few tbsps of water

Blanch romaine leaves

Remove from water and top with sauce
