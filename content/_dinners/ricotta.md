---
title: "Ricotta"
layout: recipe
servings: "2"
ingredients:
  - name: "milk"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "L"
  - name: ""
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "lemon"
  - name: "apple cider vinegar"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "salt"
    quantity: "0.5"
    unit: "tsp"

---
In a saucepan, heat milk to 180F. 

Turn off heat and add lemon juice and apple cider vinegar.

Let sit until curdles begin to form.

Strain over cheese cloth for 20min 

Add salt and let mature in fridge for 3-4 days.
