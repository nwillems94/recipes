---
title: "Chocolate Mousse (Opa's chocolademousse)"
layout: recipe
servings: "12"
ingredients:
  - name: "dark chocolate"
    quantity: "250"
    unit: "g"
  - name: "vanilla extract"
    quantity: "2"
    unit: "tsp"
  - name: "granulated sugar"
    quantity: "20"
    unit: "g"
  - name: "powdered sugar"
    quantity: "2"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "heavy cream"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "pint"
  - name: "egg whites"
    quantity: "8"
---

In a double boiler, melt the dark chocolate along with a the granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and a splash of cream. Stir until very smooth. Set aside to cool slightly.

In a second bowl beat the egg whites with a pinch of salt until stiff peaks form.

In a separate bowl beat the cream and the powdered sugar until stiff peaks form. Beat in the melted chocolate until fully combined.

Gently fold the egg whites into the chocolate-cream mixture.

Divide the mousse into individual serving dishes and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
