servings: "3"

  - name: "block Greek feta (sheep's milk)"
    quantity: "8"
    unit: "oz"
  - name: "olive oil"
    quantity: "0.5"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "cherry tomatoes"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "lb"
  - name: ""
    quantity: ""
    unit: "Basil"
  - name: "garlic"
    quantity: "6"
    unit: "cloves"

---
Combine feta, tomatoes, & garlic in baking pan. Drizzle with olive oil and black pepper.

Bake at 400 for 40 minutes.

Cook pasta. Combine with other ingredients in baking pan. Add in pasta water to create a sauce.
