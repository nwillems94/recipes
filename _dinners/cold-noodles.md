---
title: "Cold noodles"
layout: recipe
servings: "4"
ingredients:
  - name: "unseasoned rice vinegar"
    quantity: "0.33"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "vegetable oil"
    quantity: "0.33"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "Sriracha (hot chili sauce)"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "toasted sesame oil"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tbsp"
  - name: "matchstick-size pieces mixed summer vegetables (such as carrots, radishes, cucumbers, and bell peppers)"
    quantity: "8"
    unit: "cups"
  - name: "buckwheat soba (Japanese-style noodles) or vermicelli noodles"
    quantity: "8"
    unit: "ozs"
  - name: "(loosely packed) cilantro leaves with tender stems"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "scallions, thinly sliced"
    quantity: "3"
  - name: "black or white sesame seeds"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "tbsp"
---

Whisk first four ingredients in a large bowl. Add vegetables; toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper.
Cook noodles in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente; drain. Run noodles under cold water to cool them; drain well and add to bowl with vegetables. Add cilantro and scallions; season to taste with salt and pepper. Sprinkle sesame seeds over and serve.
