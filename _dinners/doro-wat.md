Hard boil the eggs. Let cool, peel, and score the sides. Set aside.

Meanwhile, season chicken with lemon and brown in oil in Dutch oven over medium heat. About 4 minutes per side. Remove and set aside

Add onions, cooking until translucent. Add tomato paste, brown, then add tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes. Add berbere and garlic and cook for additional 5 minutes

Add chicken stock to sauce. Add chicken, ensuring each piece is submerged. Flip after 5 minutes

After 5 more minutes, add hard boiled eggs. Cover again and simmer for 10 more minutes, flipping one more time

Finish sauce with butter
