---
title: "Braised short ribs"
layout: recipe
servings: "4"
ingredients:
  - name: "bone-in beef short ribs, 3 lb. boneless beef short ribs or one 3 lb. beef chuck roast, trimmed of fat"
    quantity: "4-5"
    unit: "pounds"
  - name: "white onions, sliced into ½ inch rings"
    quantity: "3"
    unit: "large"
  - name: "beef broth"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "(14oz) can"
  - name: "dry red wine"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "small Yukon gold potatoes"
    quantity: "2"
    unit: "lb"
  - name: "small carrots, peeled"
    quantity: "1"
    unit: "lb"
  - name: "water"
    quantity: "0.25"
    unit: "cup"
  - name: "flour"
    quantity: "2"
    unit: "tbsp"

---
Trim fat from meat & season with salt and pepper. Brown in Dutch oven with oil over medium heat. Drain off fat; discard. Add onions, broth, and wine.

Bring mixture to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, covered, 1 hour. 

Add whole potatoes. Return to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer, covered, 45 minutes. 

Add carrots. Cover; cook 30 minutes or until meat and vegetables are tender, spooning juices over meat and vegetables twice during cooking.

Using slotted spoon, transfer meat and vegetables to serving platter. 

In screw-top jar combine the ¼ cup water and flour, shake to combine. Add to juices in pan. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened.

Spoon over meat and vegetables.
